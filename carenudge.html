Our Approach

A HIPAA-compliant SMS system designed by clinicians to:

  • Reinforce care plans between visits
  • Flag at-risk patients early
  • Require zero staff effort

How It Works

1. Define Your Cohort

Identify patients struggling with HbA1c >9, recurrent CHF exacerbations, or poor adherence.

2. Automated Support

Patients get 3-5 weekly messages based on behavioral psychology principles.

3. Monitor Trends

Receive monthly reports on engagement and potential red flags.

Messages That Actually Work

Medication Support
“Hi Natasha!, taking your meds today helps protect your heart. Did you take your Torsemide this morning? Reply YES or NEED HELP.”

Weight Tracking
“Daily weight checks prevent 68% of CHF complications. Please reply with today’s weight in pounds. Thank you for caring for your heart!”

Gentle Activity
“A 10-minute walk after meals can lower blood sugar spikes. Want to try today? We’ll check in tonight! ”

Positive Reinforcement
“You checked your glucose 5x this week – that’s amazing progress! What helped you stay on track?”
